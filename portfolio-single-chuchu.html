Chuchu's Dual Life

Photography

I can spend 8 hours in a row in library reading a 500-page book about international relationship which, I'm sure, can bore you to death, and I can also play guitar in front of thousands of people for a whole night. Sleep? I don't have time for that.

Overview

Chuchu Liu is my best friend back in Fudan University, Shanghai, China. She is one of a few people that I truly like and admire. What I'm most interested in is that She led an impress dual life. In daytime, she is straight-A student with no makeup, who can sit quietly in library for a whole day to read some 500-page book about international relationship. However, at night, the real life begins. she will put on heavy makeup, grab her guitar and meet with her band members to perform in front of crazy crowd.

What We Have Done

  • Live with her for a week
  • Keep a record of her life with camera
  • Took almost 1,000 photos
  • Shot on 3 freezingly cold nights in a row
  • Selected and post-edited photos

Result

I put the photos together into a book, Chuchu's Dual Life, and printed it. The album has been highly praised by my photography professors, and has been exihited in a private gallery.
