Graphic Design

BACKGROUND

I began to draw posters on blackboard in high school and I found that I love it! In undergrad school, I turned to the computer. Then Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign bacame my best friends. Ever since then, I designed posters and logos for all kinds of organizations and events on campus.

EXPERIENCE

  • Designer at Fudan Spanish Association, responsible for poster, flyer and all other identity design
  • Head of Communication and Design at Fudan Photography Association
  • Freelance Designer since freshman year

TOOLS

  • DSLR camera
  • Sketch book
  • Pencils and eraser
  • Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign
