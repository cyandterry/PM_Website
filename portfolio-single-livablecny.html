LivableCNY

UI/UX Design | Web Design | Branding | Advertising

BACKGROUND

We worked as a close-knit startup company to create a brand LivableCNY. LivableCNY.com is a website that highlights everything that makes Central New York an exceptional place to live. We, several students, and our professor built the site from ground up within 3 weeks. Through monitoring Google Analytics and constantly making mistakes and experimenting, we kept refining our design, content, ads placements and SEO to improve revenue and user experience.

MY ROLE & WHAT I HAVE DONE

  • Collaborate with advertising department and social media department to create and maintain a unified brand identity across all platforms
  • Constantly collect feedbacks from users to improve user experience

ACHIEVEMENTS

The Audience

After interviews and researches, we decided that LivableCNY is targeting readers in three categories: Civic and Business Leaders, Aspirants. The following personas help to better understand the audience’s interests and needs:

POWER PLAYER

A successful individual who is highly connected across industries, the Power Player understands the importance of economic and business renewal, and takes pride in working to improve life and society around him/her.

CULTURAL ENTHUSIAST

Erudite with an abiding interest in culture and education, the Cultural Enthusiast is highly educated and holds independent, nuanced opinions, based on wide-ranging information sources tested in conversation with friends.

UPSTATE ORIGINAL

The Upstate Original has lived most of his or her life in the region, and is closely tied to the area; Central New York is home. A strong family connection motivates him/her, and while they are interested in the new, they want to preserve the original character of the area.

YOUNG CAREER SEEKER

Younger to middle age people who want to establish a career and live in Central NY. The Young Seeker focuses on family and the long term quality of life. He/She cares about community, because it is the center of every day life.

The CONTENT

All articles are written by Newhouse students. We covered every aspect of life in Central New York. LivableCNY mainly delivers content in five categories:

  • 1 Civics:
    Common sense government is a basic requirement for a livable community. In this section we celebrate the policy makers who are building sensible frameworks to keep our communities growing and viable in a changing world.
  • 2 Culture:
    Cultural richness is an important part of what makes a city livable. Central New York is blessed with visual artists, musicians, and designers who enrich the region.
  • 3 Commerce:
    A key part of a livable city is a vibrant business climate. Meaningful work makes a good life. At livableCNY, we celebrate the people and businesses bringing sustainable work to the region, and giving all of us a chance to make Central New York a livable home.
  • 4 Communities:
    Communities are the heart of the livable cities movement. Well designed public spaces bring people together to common experiences. This section is a celebration of our places.
  • 5 Connections:
    Connections celebrates the people who have come from and through the Central New York region—especially our world-class colleges and universities—and gone on to make their mark in the wider world.

The Strategy


High value content is the most important part in our strategy. Around content, we utilize social media to promote content and increase exposure; use Google Analytics to monitor website performance and user behavior; apply SEO methods to enhance searchability; embed Google AdSense to gain revenue.

SEO

We carefully organized content structure, arranged keywords on every page, optimize images sizes and quality, and chopped other unimportant features to increase SEO performance and improve user experience at the same time.
Tools We Use: Webmaster, Google Analytics, Moz

Advertising

Google AdSense and Amazon Ad Network are the platforms we use. By increasing keyword density and accuracy, we receive ads related to our content on every page. With monitoring ads performance by Google Analytics, we kept experimenting new ad placement and styles to maximize our revenue
Tools We Use: Google AdSense, Amazon Ad Network

Social Network

We have an active, organized and strategic social media plan. We not only promote our own content, but also share everything interesting about Central New York. It's a main source of our traffic.
The platforms we use are: Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Google +, Sina Weibo

The Design Process

Learn by Doing, and by Making Mistakes

Sketch Down the IDea

In this project, from the very beginning, my professor and I sketched down and went over every move of our design, from ideation, scenario, persona, strategy, to logo design, website structure, section wireframes, and page layout. From constantly review my design process, I got lot of inspiration. Also in this way, it makes the design sustainable and extensible. Thus, It was a great experience to think and sketch the structure of the whole site from the very beginning.




Weekly Meeting, Priority and Deadline

I, as the head of UI/UX, worked with everyone on team. I worked with the programmer to turn design prototype into reality, with editor to adjust article layout and optimize images, with advertising team to design ad styles and experiment with different placement, with social media team to designed unified brand image across all platforms.

In the process, to facilitate our workflow and improve efficiency, we set up weekly and monthly goals for both individuals and the group. Also, every day at 10:00AM and 2:00PM, we have group meetings in which we made presentations about our progress and state of work to everyone. In this way, everyone's responsibility and the priority is clearly addressed, which helps us keep high efficiency all through the process.



Let Data Talk

Although we're experimenting all the time, we made every change based on Google Analytics report. I openly collect feedbacks from my teammates and current users, and refine the design after discussion. For example, when I changed the placement of the ads, we'll then closely monitoring the Google Analytics for a week and see whether we gain better results. In this way, I make sure every move is meaningful and valuable.
