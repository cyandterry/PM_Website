Product Design for WonderGirls Makeup

User Research | Product Design

Background

We were assigned by Unilever to develop new product for kids from 8 to 15 in New Producnt Development class. After rounds of brainstorm and idea-screening, we decided to develop a new makeup brand designed specifically for tween girls. From interviews, we discovered untapped makeup market for tween girls. With Unilever's prestigious reputation in personal care, and existing brands' advanced technology, we found this idea doable and profitable.

Market Research

Market Size & Market Share

In America, There are currently 20 million tweens in the U.S. and they are projected to hit 23 million by 2020 (U.S. Census), which is a huge untapped demographics for cosmetics; For the whole cosmetic marekt, beauty supply, and perfume store (beauty store) industry includes about 15,000 stores with combined annual revenue of about $13 billion. As time advances, more young girls will have the need at earlier age.

Why for Tween Girls?

They will 'steal' their mothers' makeup

  • Strong puberty needs: too old for toys and too early for boys; heavily influenced by peers; friends and their status is everything to them
  • Hate that she's too young for too many things: She wants to be anything but the age she is, always looking toward the future, is ambitious
  • Immersed and influenced by pop culture

An Untapped Market

  • No market dominator: too many small brands, no big brands target this group
  • Great need to educate the tween girls
  • Existing brands are for fun, or of low quality
  • Skincare products dominate the main market for young girls
  • Parents care about whether the ingredients are eco-friendly and natural

Competitor Analysis

No Market Dominator

From our research, we found that there are almost no well-known Cosmetics brands that are designed specifically for young girls. Most brands are designed for adults which have mature colors and tools (pencil or brush) that are hard for them to use. For girls aged 8 to 15, skincare dominates this market.

Natural ingredients is the biggest concern

Parent hope the products are made of all-natural ingredients that won't harm their little princesses' skin. We discovered that almost all competitors are promoting natural, eco-friendly ingredients, but most of them, from package design to ads, leave people with low-quality impression, and are not truly, strictly all-natural.

Make Them Beautiful as They Are

Some girl's makeup brands make girls look too mature. There aren't much difference between their products and those for adults. Therefore, for WonderGirls, we will design specifically and whole-heartedly for tween girls. With fully considering their needs and limits, we will design suitable colors and tools for them to make them beautiful as they already are.

Interviews and Customer Insights

The Dilemma

After interviews, we find that almost all tween girls have a great interest in makeup. However, many parents won't allow any makeup more than lip gloss on them until high school. Even so, girls will steal their mothers' makeup to satisfy their curiosity and desire . So there is great confrontation between user and buyer, and banning is not the solution

Only Basic Products

Many parents said that they will only allow their daughters to use lip gloss, blush and some mild color eye shadows. Those of wierd color, or products need to be used with pens or anything that might poke eyes, are not allowed. They hope these basic products can make girls naturally beautiful.

Special Occasions are Allowed

For special events at school like dancing or chorus, girls need to put on makeup. This is the time that parents will allow their daughters to put on rather heavy makeup. If school won't do it for them, mothers have to do it themselves. We found it a very good opportunity for us to not only provide products, but also provide professional makeup service for tween girls.

Solutions & Strategy


Hurdle 1: Too many small Brands, no industry lead.
Our Solution: Unilever’s brand equity and expertise on skincare

Hurdle 2: Parents’ dilemma of Sexualisation concern and good parenting skill
Our Solution: Partner with trustworthy beauty stores like Sephora to provide professional makeup service to tween girls

Hurdle 3: “I want my tween girl to feel good and beautiful. That’s important.”
Our Solution: WonderGirls provides mini pack of basic Cosmetics, a perfect choice for her natural beauty.
