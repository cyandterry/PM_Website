Fudamni App Design

UI/UX Design | Branding

Fudamni = Fudan + Alumni.
It is an app for Fudan Alumni Association.

The mockups of welcome page, homepage and events page of the app.

After research and several rounds of brainstorms, I drafted the functions and initial wireframes as above.

I sketched the wireframes and designed the user flow. After discussing with teammates and talking to several potential users, I have made changes to the initial design.

You can download the user flow here.

Background

Fudamni app is my first experience of UI/UX design during undergraduate years. We built an mobile app for Fudan Alumni Association within three months.

Overview

This is project assigned to us when we were working with Fudan University Alumni Association for over 3 months. I led a team of 6 to design an integrated communication campaign to help promote Fudan Alumni Association. This app is a significant part of the campaign.

From conducting research online, delievering 500+ surveys, interviewing students, alumnis and school officials, to brainstorming, sketching wireframes, making mockups, to final prototype, we finally designed the app Fudamni to benefit both current students and Fudan Alumni around the world.
