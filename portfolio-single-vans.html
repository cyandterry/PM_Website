Vans Print Ads Design

Graphic Design, Branding, Advertising, Photography

Overview

This is a project for the class Photography in Advertising. We were assigned to take a series of photos for clients to best represent their brand image and value proposition. Through several rounds of discussion, we decided to pick Vans. One reason is that, all of us were fond of street culture. And as a photographer, I once shot street snaps for Vans, so it was easy for me to ask them for sponsorship.

After brainstorming, we discovered that, in our mind, Vans means young, bold, wild, free and everything related to street culture. People who love Vans are confident to be themselves. So we came up with the tagline 'Be Yourself', 'Be Bold', and 'Be Wild'. To convey the ideas, we searched for models and cool places to shoot around Shanghai. After two days' hard work, here are what we have.

What We Have Done

  • Market research
  • Brainstorm ideas
  • Search for models
  • Ask for sponsorship
  • Shoot photos
  • Post-edited photos

Result

The final project was ranked one of the best in class. It also received over thousands of views and hundreds of share on social media.
